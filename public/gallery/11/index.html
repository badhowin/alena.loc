Sea, sing me your song

Sea, sing me your song Sea, sing me your song
And I’ll sing you my own.
About things which don’t wound me
Any more.
How everything slips away.
How I’m crumbling and you
Remain the same.
Sea, cradle me in your arms.
I will sleep quietly.
Splinters of glass don’t wound, sea.
Sing me your song.

May 2018
